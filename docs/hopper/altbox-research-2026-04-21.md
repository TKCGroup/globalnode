# Altbox research — altbot.ai (captured 2026-04-21)

## Positioning

- **Core tagline:** "Your AI. Now Local."
- **Category framing:** "a private AI compute hub that runs your agents locally"
- **Core claim:** "All processing moves local" with "zero cloud dependency"

## Specs & price

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Price | $799 (pre-order) |
| Ship date | Q3 2026 |
| Compute | 67 TOPS |
| Chipset | Jetson Orin Nano Super (1024 CUDA cores, 15W TDP) |
| Max power | 25W |
| Connectivity | Zigbee + Matter |

## Marketing claims worth reusing / adapting

- **The "gap" hook:**
  > "ChatGPT doesn't know your home. Alexa doesn't have a brain. Home Assistant doesn't have an agent. Alt has all three."
- **The onboarding flow:**
  > "Download your trained agent onto your Altbox. Day 1 ready — no cold start, no learning period."
- **Privacy / ownership framing:**
  > "Your data stays yours," "$0/month"

## Tone & visual

- Clean, minimalist.
- Emphasis on privacy + local control.
- Language emphasizes data ownership.
- Tech-forward but consumer-friendly; avoids jargon while keeping sophistication.

## Companion product structure

- **Altbot** (cloud) = the trainer. Users train agents free in the cloud.
- **Altbox** (device) = the destination. Users transfer trained agents to Altbox hardware.

## Cross-promo angle for globalnode.ai

The natural bridge: altbot.ai sells Altbox as **local compute for your agents**. globalnode.ai reframes Altbox as **the Node, fabricated** — the physical substrate your GAM lives on. Both are true; we just name the layers.

Candidate hero copy (drafted in the `cell-node-wire-visual.html` Altbox section — see hopper copy):

- Headline: **"A Node you can hold."**
- Rhythm echo: "Your AI. Now Local." ↔ "Your Node. Now Real."
- Rhetorical close, adapted from altbot's own hook:
  > "ChatGPT doesn't know your Cells. Alexa doesn't speak the Wire. A Node without hardware is a diagram. **Altbox is the Node, made real.**"

Three benefit cards translated from altbot's consumer framing into Node-speak:
- "Your Cells never leave the box" (privacy → Passport-gated)
- "Runs your agents locally" (verbatim altbot claim + Day-1 ready)
- "Speaks the Wire out of the box" (Consulate pre-loaded + Zigbee/Matter carryover)
