# globalnode — LONG-TERM

> Months out · vision · brain dumps · "someday" / "wouldn't it be cool if" / "eventually."

## The long arc

This landing page is *the visible tip* of a multi-year protocol effort:

- **Now:** one-page manifesto + waitlist + cross-promo to Altbox (altbot.ai).
- **6 months out:** Consulate reference implementation runs on a real Altbox; demo video replaces the stylized diagram on the homepage. "Run a Consulate" CTA links to a working install.
- **12 months out:** Wire v0.1 spec published at `spec.globalnode.ai`. Second Consulate (not ours) runs in the wild. Mutual Pulse exchange demonstrated on video.
- **24 months out:** Altbox ships Q3 2026 per altbot.ai commitment; globalnode.ai pivots from "manifesto" to "buy + boot + federate."
- **36 months+:** other ecosystems (Anthropic? HuggingFace? an ActivityPub bridge?) speak the Wire. "The agent internet" is visible, not theoretical.

## Vision fragments

### On Altbox as a Node
An Altbox on every founder's desk, one Cell per agent, Wire between them. Tyler at his MBP sends a Pulse to a fellow founder's Altbox about a proposal; their Consulate routes it to their research agent; 20 seconds later Tyler has a response. No account. No platform. No advertiser reading it. That's the dream.

### On the brand surviving its technical stage
Signal is boring to look at, technical to read, and widely loved. Its landing page looks almost identical to 2014 because the promise — "encrypted messaging, no custodian" — hasn't changed and didn't need redressing. globalnode could age the same way: the brand doesn't evolve much because the commitments don't.

### On what the Wire opens up
Not obvious from the current landing: **the GAN unlocks agent-to-agent markets.** An agent on Node A wants a service an agent on Node B provides; Pulse + payment + response. No Stripe, no account, no login. That's a future landing section; skip for v1.

### On who this is for
Not "AI enthusiasts." Not "crypto people." People who care about owning their infrastructure. Same Venn as Signal users + self-hosted homelabers + developers who still think the web was better before platform-capture. Small audience; strong audience. The landing copy should not try to broaden it.

## Wouldn't it be cool if

- **Every Altbox has a physical mark** — the three-ring brand etched into the top surface. Branding as object.
- **The landing page loads with a single Pulse animation** fired through the diagram as the first visitor arrives — live on every load. A new Cell, a new Node, every time.
- **A public Wire telemetry page** (Quiet Mode off, voluntary) showing aggregate Pulses/hour across participating Nodes. Like a flag count on the Apollo page.
- **Physical Passport cards** — business-card-size NFC/QR tokens that hand a peer your Node's contact card. "Tap to federate." Conference swag that's also the protocol.
- **Altbox + weather station integration** — the 06:00 Pulse to your agents rides the same LAN as the thermostat. Full-stack home intelligence.
- **A children's-book version of Cell / Node / Wire** — teaching infrastructure to kids the same way "Everybody Poops" teaches biology. "Every agent is a Cell. Every house is a Node. The Wire is how the Nodes whisper."
